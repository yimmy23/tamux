# Operations Process Task Guideline

Operational work should reduce ambiguity and handoff failure.

## Workflow

1. Identify current process, actors, inputs, outputs, systems, and pain points.
2. Map the workflow from trigger to completion.
3. Separate required controls from historical habit.
4. Use `process-docs` or `internal-docs` for SOPs/runbooks, `project-execution` for delivery plans, `remote-collaboration` for distributed teams, and `support-docs` for customer-facing help.
5. Create checklists or SOPs that are usable during real work.
6. Include measurement: cycle time, quality, cost, risk, or throughput.

## Quality Gate

Do not document an ideal process without checking how work actually flows today.
