# Marketing Campaign Task Guideline

Campaign work should connect audience, message, channel, and measurement.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, offer, funnel stage, campaign goal, budget, timeline, and constraints.
2. Define the core message and proof points before writing assets.
3. Match channel strategy to audience behavior and campaign objective; use channel-specific skills for lifecycle email, ads, landing pages, launch planning, tracking, and experiments.
4. Create variants for distinct segments or channels instead of one generic message.
5. Define success metrics, tracking plan, and learning agenda.
6. Include launch checklist, approvals, and post-campaign review.

## Quality Gate

Do not produce campaign assets before the target audience, offer, and measurement plan are clear.
