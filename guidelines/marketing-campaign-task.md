## Overview

Marketing campaigns need clear goals, defined audiences, and measurable results.

## Workflow

1. Define campaign goals and KPIs before creative work.
2. Identify target audience segments and appropriate channels.
3. Develop messaging and creative assets for each channel.
4. Set up tracking and measurement before launch.
5. Test assumptions with a launch validation phase.
6. Monitor performance and adjust mid-campaign.
7. Report results against goals with lessons learned.

## Quality Gate

A campaign is ready when goals, audience, channels, and measurement are defined.
