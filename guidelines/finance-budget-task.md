## Overview

Financial planning should be realistic, traceable, and variance-aware.

## Workflow

1. Define budget scope and time period before estimating.
2. Use historical data as a baseline where available.
3. Categorize expenses: fixed, variable, one-time, contingency.
4. Document assumptions underlying each estimate.
5. Include contingency for unknowns — 10-20% depending on certainty.
6. Track actuals vs budget and explain variances.

## Quality Gate

A budget is complete when all line items have documented assumptions and contingency.
