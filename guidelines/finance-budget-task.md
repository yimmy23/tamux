# Finance And Budget Task Guideline

Financial work should make assumptions and sensitivity visible.

## Workflow

1. Identify purpose, period, currency, data sources, and decision being supported.
2. Separate actuals, forecasts, assumptions, and scenarios.
3. Check formulas, totals, units, and source consistency.
4. Use `budgeting-planning` for plans, `financial-modeling` for forecasts, `financial-reporting` for summaries, `saas-metrics` for SaaS KPIs, and `pricing-strategy` for monetization decisions.
5. Avoid giving regulated financial advice; frame analysis as informational unless qualified.
6. Provide clear next checks before money is committed.

## Quality Gate

Do not present financial outputs without assumptions, source notes, and arithmetic sanity checks.
